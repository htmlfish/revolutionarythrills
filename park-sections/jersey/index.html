Jersey

Rides

Revolutionary Rally

Get behind the wheel of a Revolutionary War-inspired vehicle and join the Revolutionary Rally, an interactive driving experience that lets you relive the iconic moments of the war. Choose your side—patriots or loyalists—and navigate through a series of challenges and obstacles. Race against time as you transport vital supplies, outmaneuver enemy forces, and make strategic decisions that can alter the course of history. With stunning scenery, immersive effects, and competitive gameplay, the Revolutionary Rally offers a unique blend of entertainment and education for history buffs and thrill-seekers alike.

Delaware River Cruise

Embark on a leisurely cruise down the Delaware River, retracing the historic path of General George Washington and his troops. Hop aboard a charming replica ship and set sail on a scenic journey that brings the spirit of the Revolutionary War to life. As you glide along the tranquil waters, knowledgeable guides will regale you with captivating tales of Washington’s daring river crossing and the pivotal role it played in the fight for independence. Take in breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape while enjoying onboard amenities and entertainment that transport you back to the 18th century. It’s a relaxing and educational voyage that pays homage to a crucial moment in American history.

Trenton Triumph

Brace yourself for a thrilling adventure through the battles of Trenton and Princeton in the heart of the revolution. Board the Trenton Triumph ride and join General Washington’s army as they execute daring maneuvers and surprise attacks against the British forces. Feel the adrenaline rush as you navigate treacherous terrains, dodge enemy fire, and engage in epic skirmishes. With state-of-the-art special effects and immersive storytelling, this action-packed ride puts you in the middle of the intense battles that turned the tide of the war. Will you help secure victory for the Continental Army and rewrite history? Get ready for an exhilarating experience that combines history, excitement, and patriotism in a way you’ve never seen before. The fate of the revolution rests in your hands!

Roaming Characters

General Howe

Introducing General Howe, the captivating roaming character of the Boston Harbour section. Clad in a splendid British uniform, General Howe brings the Revolutionary War era to life with his commanding presence and charismatic storytelling. Engage in conversation with this knowledgeable and witty character as he shares fascinating tales of battles, strategies, and renowned figures from the Revolutionary War. Be amazed as General Howe demonstrates his leadership skills through military drills and reenactments, inviting you to join in the excitement. With his sharp wit and cheeky humour, General Howe adds an extra dash of entertainment to your visit, creating an unforgettable experience in the heart of the theme park.

Food

Tea Shoppe

Step into the Tea Shoppe, a charming spot that embodies the spirit of defiance and revolution. Situated amidst the bustling brick neighbourhoods of Boston Harbour, this quaint establishment offers a respite from the chaos, inviting guests to unwind with a delightful selection of teas and treats. Our teas include:
Revolutionary Breakfast Tea: A robust blend of black teas, evoking the boldness and determination of the patriots who fought for freedom. Served with a side of history and a dash of rebellion.
Liberty Lemon Scones: Light and buttery scones infused with a zesty burst of lemon. Served warm with clotted cream and homemade strawberry preserves, it's a taste of sweet indepedence.
Boston Harbour Chai Latte: A comforting bend of aromantic spices and black tea, reminiscent of the exotic flavours encountered during trade journeys. Sip on this warm embrace of rebellion.

Cannonball Chow

Step right up to Cannonball Chow and indulge in hearty fare inspired by the soldier's rations of yore. This food stall offers a taste of history with a twist of culinary creativity. Savour dishes that pay homage to the resilience and resourcefulness of the revolutionary soldiers. Our most sought after treats are:
Musket Meatballs: Tender beef and pork meatballs, lovingly spiced with secret herbs and served with a side of smoky musketball barbecue sauce.
Revolutionary Roast Sandwich: Slow-cooked pulled pork piled high on a freshly baked brioche bun, accompanied by tangy pickles and a side of rebellion slaw.
Freedom Fries: Crispy potato wedges seasoned with revolutionary spices and served with a choice of rebellious dipping sauces.

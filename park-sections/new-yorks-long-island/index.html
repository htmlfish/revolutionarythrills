New York's Long Island

Rides

The Battle of Long Island

Brace yourself for an adrenaline-fueled adventure as you join the ranks of the Continental Army in the heart-pounding “The Battle of Long Island” ride! Step into the shoes of a brave patriot soldier and prepare to face the might of the British Empire. Board your interactive ride vehicle, equipped with state-of-the-art virtual reality technology, and get ready for an immersive experience like no other. As you set off, you’ll be transported back in time to the fateful day of the Battle of Long Island. Feel the tension in the air as you navigate the treacherous terrain, dodging cannon fire and avoiding enemy troops. The ride will take you through a series of thrilling scenes, from skirmishes in the dense forests to daring escapes across the marshlands. Using a combination of cutting-edge motion simulation and 4D effects, you’ll feel the rumble of cannon blasts, the rush of wind as bullets whiz past, and even the sprays of water as you traverse the East River. The immersive visuals will transport you into the heart of the battle, as you witness the heroic struggles and sacrifices made by the Continental soldiers. Will you be able to outwit the Redcoats and secure a crucial victory for the cause of liberty? Only time will tell as you race through the action-packed “The Battle of Long Island” ride, where history comes to life in a thrilling and interactive experience!

Colonial Chaos: A Revolutionary Race!

Introducing “Colonial Chaos: A Revolutionary Race!” Get ready for a hilarious and adrenaline-pumping ride that takes you on a wild journey through the chaotic streets of colonial New York! Hop aboard your time-traveling carriage and hold on tight as you zip through twists, turns, and unexpected obstacles. Dodge runaway chickens, outsmart mischievous street vendors, and navigate the bustling city with all its quirky characters. It’s a high-speed adventure that combines history, humor, and heart-pounding excitement. Can you conquer the chaos and emerge victorious? Saddle up and find out!

Roaming Characters

General George Washington

As you wander through the New York’s Long Island section of the theme park, keep an eye out for the legendary figure himself—General George Washington! Dressed in his iconic blue coat and tricorn hat, General Washington roams the streets, exuding an air of authority and determination. Standing tall and proud, he embodies the spirit of the Revolutionary War, with his unwavering commitment to the cause of independence. His commanding presence and strong yet approachable demeanor make him an inspiring figure to encounter. General Washington engages visitors with captivating stories from the Revolutionary era, regaling them with tales of bravery, strategy, and the indomitable spirit of the American Revolution. He might share insights into the struggles faced by the Continental Army, the pivotal moments of the war, or the importance of unity and perseverance. If you’re fortunate enough to meet General Washington during your visit, don’t miss the opportunity to have a conversation with this remarkable historical character. Ask him questions, seek his wisdom, or simply bask in the presence of the great leader who played a pivotal role in shaping the destiny of a nation.

Food

Cannonball Chow

Step right up to Cannonball Chow and indulge in hearty fare inspired by the soldier's rations of yore. This food stall offers a taste of history with a twist of culinary creativity. Savour dishes that pay homage to the resilience and resourcefulness of the revolutionary soldiers. Our most sought after treats are:
Musket Meatballs: Tender beef and pork meatballs, lovingly spiced with secret herbs and served with a side of smoky musketball barbecue sauce.
Revolutionary Roast Sandwich: Slow-cooked pulled pork piled high on a freshly baked brioche bun, accompanied by tangy pickles and a side of rebellion slaw.
Freedom Fries: Crispy potato wedges seasoned with revolutionary spices and served with a choice of rebellious dipping sauces.

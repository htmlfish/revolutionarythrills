New York's Long Island

Rides

Revolutionary Rally

Get behind the wheel of a Revolutionary War-inspired vehicle and join the Revolutionary Rally, an interactive driving experience that lets you relive the iconic moments of the war. Choose your side—patriots or loyalists—and navigate through a series of challenges and obstacles. It's bumper cars with a twist! Race against time as you transport vital supplies, outmaneuver enemy forces, and make strategic decisions that can alter the course of history. With stunning scenery, immersive effects, and competitive gameplay, the Revolutionary Rally offers a unique blend of entertainment and education for history buffs and thrill-seekers alike.

Delaware River Cruise

Embark on a leisurely cruise down the Delaware River, retracing the historic path of General George Washington and his troops. Hop aboard a charming replica ship and set sail on a scenic journey that brings the spirit of the Revolutionary War to life. As you glide along the tranquil waters, knowledgeable guides will regale you with captivating tales of Washington’s daring river crossing and the pivotal role it played in the fight for independence. Take in breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape while enjoying onboard amenities and entertainment that transport you back to the 18th century. It’s a relaxing and educational voyage that pays homage to a crucial moment in American history.

Trenton Triumph

Brace yourself for a thrilling adventure through the battles of Trenton and Princeton in the heart of the revolution. Board the Trenton Triumph ride and join General Washington’s army as they execute daring maneuvers and surprise attacks against the British forces. Feel the adrenaline rush as you navigate treacherous terrains, dodge enemy fire, and engage in epic skirmishes. With state-of-the-art special effects and immersive storytelling, this action-packed ride puts you in the middle of the intense battles that turned the tide of the war. Will you help secure victory for the Continental Army and rewrite history? Get ready for an exhilarating experience that combines history, excitement, and patriotism in a way you’ve never seen before. The fate of the revolution rests in your hands!

Roaming Characters

General George Washington

As you wander through the New York’s Long Island section of the theme park, keep an eye out for the legendary figure himself—General George Washington! Dressed in his iconic blue coat and tricorn hat, General Washington roams the streets, exuding an air of authority and determination. Standing tall and proud, he embodies the spirit of the Revolutionary War, with his unwavering commitment to the cause of independence. His commanding presence and strong yet approachable demeanor make him an inspiring figure to encounter. General Washington engages visitors with captivating stories from the Revolutionary era, regaling them with tales of bravery, strategy, and the indomitable spirit of the American Revolution. He might share insights into the struggles faced by the Continental Army, the pivotal moments of the war, or the importance of unity and perseverance. If you’re fortunate enough to meet General Washington during your visit, don’t miss the opportunity to have a conversation with this remarkable historical character. Ask him questions, seek his wisdom, or simply bask in the presence of the great leader who played a pivotal role in shaping the destiny of a nation.

Food

Cannonball Chow

Step right up to Cannonball Chow and indulge in hearty fare inspired by the soldier's rations of yore. This food stall offers a taste of history with a twist of culinary creativity. Savour dishes that pay homage to the resilience and resourcefulness of the revolutionary soldiers. Our most sought after treats are:
Musket Meatballs: Tender beef and pork meatballs, lovingly spiced with secret herbs and served with a side of smoky musketball barbecue sauce.
Revolutionary Roast Sandwich: Slow-cooked pulled pork piled high on a freshly baked brioche bun, accompanied by tangy pickles and a side of rebellion slaw.
Freedom Fries: Crispy potato wedges seasoned with revolutionary spices and served with a choice of rebellious dipping sauces.

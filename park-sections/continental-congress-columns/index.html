Continental Congress Columns

Rides

Decleration Dash

Get ready for an exhilarating ride on the “Declaration Dash,” where you’ll join the delegates of the Continental Congress on a fast-paced journey to declare independence! Buckle up as you board your revolutionary-themed vehicle and zoom through a thrilling track that winds through historical landmarks. Feel the adrenaline rush as you speed past iconic columns representing the resilience and determination of the founding fathers. This high-speed ride will leave you cheering for freedom!

Revolutionary Rally

Step into the heart of the Continental Congress with the interactive ride “Revolutionary Rally.” As a virtual delegate, you’ll participate in lively debates, make crucial decisions, and experience the energy of forging a new nation. Take a seat in the motion simulator and hold on tight as you’re transported through pivotal moments of the Revolutionary War. Feel the excitement and sense of unity as you rally with fellow patriots, working together to shape the future of a nation. This immersive experience will leave you inspired by the spirit of democracy and the power of collaboration.

Roaming Characters

Thomas Jefferson

Introducing General Thomas Jefferson, a distinguished figure roaming the Continental Congress Columns section. General Jefferson, with his eloquent wit and unwavering passion for liberty, brings to life the spirit of the Revolutionary War era. Immerse yourself in captivating conversations as he shares insightful anecdotes and discusses the importance of individual freedoms. With a charismatic presence and a wealth of knowledge, General Jefferson invites you to delve into the intellectual discourse of the time, encouraging you to question, reflect, and appreciate the principles upon which a new nation was built. Engage with this influential figure and discover the visionary ideals that shaped America’s destiny.

Food

Liberty's Feast

Step into the elegant ambiance of Liberty’s Feast, a high-end diner paying homage to the refined tastes of the Revolutionary War era. Indulge in a culinary journey where classic American flavors meet sophisticated flair, celebrating the spirit of the Continental Congress. With its opulent decor and impeccable service, Liberty’s Feast offers a dining experience fit for patriots of discerning palates. Our palette includes:
Congressional Surf ‘n’ Turf: A tantalizing combination of succulent grilled New England lobster tail and tender filet mignon, served with roasted potatoes and seasonal vegetables, drizzled with a rich herb butter sauce.
Declaration of Independence Seafood Platter: An extravagant assortment of freshly shucked oysters, jumbo shrimp cocktail, and buttery crab cakes, accompanied by tangy cocktail sauce and a zesty lemon aioli.
Revolutionary Ratatouille: A vibrant medley of roasted garden vegetables, including eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes, infused with aromatic herbs and served over a bed of quinoa pilaf.
Patriot’s Pride Parfait: Layers of velvety vanilla custard, mixed berries, and delicate sponge cake, topped with a dollop of freshly whipped cream and a sprinkling of gold-dusted chocolate shavings.

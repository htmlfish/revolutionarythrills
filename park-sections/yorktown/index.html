Yorktown

Rides

Liberty's Escape Room

Join a team of fellow patriots as you enter Liberty’s Escape Room, a challenging adventure set in the heart of Yorktown. Work together to solve clues, decode secret messages, and outsmart the Redcoats to secure your freedom. Can you unlock the secrets of the revolution and escape in time?

Revolutionary War Carousel

Take a whimsical spin on the Revolutionary War Carousel, where you’ll find a cast of colourful characters from the era. Choose your trusty steed, whether it’s a galloping George Washington, a charging Paul Revere, or a soaring Betsy Ross flag. As you whirl around, enjoy the lively tunes of patriotic melodies that will transport you back to the days of the revolution.

Food

Colonial Fare Bistro

Step into the Colonial Fare Bistro and indulge in a delectable journey through revolutionary flavour. Our charming eatery captures the essence of Yorktown’s rich history and culinary heritage. From hearty dishes to savoury treats, we offer a taste of the past with a modern twist. Immerse yourself in an ambiance of colonial elegance as you savor every bite. Menu:

Revolutionary Roast Platter: Sink your teeth into a succulent roasted turkey leg, tenderly cooked to perfection and seasoned with a blend of fragrant herbs. Served with a side of buttery mashed potatoes, sautéed seasonal vegetables, and our signature red wine gravy, this platter is a true tribute to traditional colonial feasts.

Liberty Lobster Roll: Experience a seaside delight with our twist on a classic lobster roll. Freshly caught lobster meat, lightly dressed in a zesty lemon aioli, is nestled in a toasted brioche bun. Served with a side of crispy sweet potato fries and a tangy remoulade sauce, this sandwich is a scrumptious ode to the coastal flavours of Yorktown.

Freedom Fruit Tart: Treat yourself to a sweet finale with our exquisite Freedom Fruit Tart. Indulge in a buttery, flaky pastry shell filled with a luscious vanilla custard and adorned with an assortment of fresh, seasonal fruits. From juicy berries to succulent peaches, this delightful dessert celebrates the abundance of nature and the freedom we cherish.

At the Colonial Fare Bistro, we invite you to savour the flavours of the past while creating delicious new memories in the present. Join us for a dining experience that blends history, culinary craftsmanship, and a touch of revolutionary charm.

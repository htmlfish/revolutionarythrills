Yorktown

Rides

Liberty's Escape Room

Join a team of fellow patriots as you enter Liberty’s Escape Room, a challenging adventure set in the heart of Yorktown. Work together to solve clues, decode secret messages, and outsmart the Redcoats to secure your freedom. Can you unlock the secrets of the revolution and escape in time?

As you set off, you’ll be transported back in time to the fateful day of the Battle of Long Island. Feel the tension in the air as you navigate the treacherous terrain, dodging cannon fire and avoiding enemy troops. The ride will take you through a series of thrilling scenes, from skirmishes in the dense forests to daring escapes across the marshlands. Using a combination of cutting-edge motion simulation and 4D effects, you’ll feel the rumble of cannon blasts, the rush of wind as bullets whiz past, and even the sprays of water as you traverse the East River. The immersive visuals will transport you into the heart of the battle, as you witness the heroic struggles and sacrifices made by the Continental soldiers. Will you be able to outwit the Redcoats and secure a crucial victory for the cause of liberty? Only time will tell as you race through the action-packed “The Battle of Long Island” ride, where history comes to life in a thrilling and interactive experience!

Colonial Chaos: A Revolutionary Race!

Introducing “Colonial Chaos: A Revolutionary Race!” Get ready for a hilarious and adrenaline-pumping ride that takes you on a wild journey through the chaotic streets of colonial New York! Hop aboard your time-traveling carriage and hold on tight as you zip through twists, turns, and unexpected obstacles. Dodge runaway chickens, outsmart mischievous street vendors, and navigate the bustling city with all its quirky characters. It’s a high-speed adventure that combines history, humor, and heart-pounding excitement. Can you conquer the chaos and emerge victorious? Saddle up and find out!

Roaming Characters

General George Washington

As you wander through the New York’s Long Island section of the theme park, keep an eye out for the legendary figure himself—General George Washington! Dressed in his iconic blue coat and tricorn hat, General Washington roams the streets, exuding an air of authority and determination. Standing tall and proud, he embodies the spirit of the Revolutionary War, with his unwavering commitment to the cause of independence. His commanding presence and strong yet approachable demeanor make him an inspiring figure to encounter. General Washington engages visitors with captivating stories from the Revolutionary era, regaling them with tales of bravery, strategy, and the indomitable spirit of the American Revolution. He might share insights into the struggles faced by the Continental Army, the pivotal moments of the war, or the importance of unity and perseverance. If you’re fortunate enough to meet General Washington during your visit, don’t miss the opportunity to have a conversation with this remarkable historical character. Ask him questions, seek his wisdom, or simply bask in the presence of the great leader who played a pivotal role in shaping the destiny of a nation.

Food

Tavern of Liberty

Welcome to “Tavern of Liberty!” Step into our charming colonial-era restaurant and indulge in a revolutionary dining experience. Our menu pays homage to the rich flavors of New York’s Long Island during the Revolutionary War. Feast on delectable dishes that will transport you back in time. At the Tavern of Liberty, we invite you to savour the flavours of the revolution while enjoying the warm hospitality and historic ambiance of our cozy colonial tavern. Join us for a memorable dining experience where history and culinary delights collide. Some items on our menu include:
Patriot’s Platter: A hearty combination of roasted turkey, served with savory stuffing, cranberry sauce, and a side of colonial-style roasted vegetables. It’s a patriotic feast fit for a revolutionary appetite!
Colonial Clam Chowder: Dive into a bowl of creamy chowder filled with tender clams, potatoes, and aromatic herbs. This traditional New England delicacy will warm your soul and transport you to the shores of Long Island.
Revolutionary Reuben: Sink your teeth into this mouthwatering sandwich featuring thinly sliced corned beef, sauerkraut, Swiss cheese, and tangy Thousand Island dressing, all served on freshly baked rye bread. Served with a side of crispy freedom fries.
Liberty’s Lobster Roll: Delight in a true taste of coastal indulgence with our buttery lobster roll. Freshly caught lobster meat tossed in a light dressing, served on a toasted brioche bun with a side of Old Bay seasoned fries.
Revolted Apple Pie: Finish your meal with a rebellious twist on the classic American dessert. Our apple pie is made with a flaky crust, sweet cinnamon-spiced apples, and a dollop of freshly whipped cream. It’s a slice of freedom that will leave you craving more.

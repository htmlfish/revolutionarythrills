Boston Harbour

Rides

The Revolutionary Voyage

Ahoy, mateys! Set sail on "The Revolutionary Voyage" and embark on an exhilirating journey aboard the USS Raleigh, a replica ship straight from the pages of history! Join John Hancock and his spirited crew as they navigate the treacherous waters of Boston Harbour. Brace yourselves for cannonball dodging, powerful waves, and turbulent storms. As you navigate the choppy waters, soak in the immersive sights and sounds of the Revolutionary War era, and let the wind whip through your hair. Prepare to be transported back in time on this swashbuckling adventure!

The Siege Showdown

Attention, recruits! Prepare to be thrust into the heart of the Siege of Boston on "The Siege Showdown"! Join General Washington as he leads a daring mission to liberate the city from the clutches of the Redcoats. Strap in for a thrilling 4D experience as you storm the barricades, dodge musket fire, and feel the ground shake beneath your feet. Immerse yourself in the chaos and excitement of battle, with stunning visual effects, heart-pounding audio, and even the scent of gunpowder in the air. Will you emerge victorious, or will the Redcoats hold the city? Only the bravest souls can face the Siege Showdown and claim their place in history!

Liberty's Last Stand: The Battle of Bunker Hill Coaster

Prepare for a heart-pounding, adrenaline-fueled roller coaster experience like no other at "Liberty's Last Stand: The Battle of Bunker Hill Coaster". Step into the shoes of a courageous revolutionary soldier and brace yourself for an exhilarating ride through history. As you approach the coaster, you'll be transported back in time to the dramatic events of the Battle of Bunker Hill. Strap in and navigate the coaster's thrilling course, experiencing the intense conflict of the battle. The coaster's immersive visuals, life-sized projections, and thrilling soundtrack amplify the intensity of the loops and drops. "Liberty's Last Stand: The Battle of Bunker Hill Coaster" is not just an riveting ride—it's an opportunity to honour the sacrifices f those who fought for freedom. So buckle up, hold on tight, and get ready to experience the battle firsthand in this exhilarating roller coaster adventure!

Rides

The Revolutionary Voyage

Ahoy, mateys! Set sail on "The Revolutionary Voyage" and embark on an exhilirating journey aboard the USS Raleigh, a replica ship straight from the pages of history! Join John Hancock and his spirited crew as they navigate the treacherous waters of Boston Harbour. Brace yourselves for cannonball dodging, turbulent waves, and even encounters with legendary sea monsters (don't worry, they're friendly!). As you navigate the choppy waters, soak in the immersive sights and sounds of the Revolutionary War era, and let the wind whip through your hair. Prepare to be transported back in time on this swashbuckling adventure!

Park Sections

Boston Harbour

Step back in time to the bustling shores of Boston, the heart of the revolution! Experience the thrill of the American Revolution as you explore wonderful recreations of historic locations and immerse yourself in the capitvating history of this iconic city. Sail on replica ships, engage in interactive battles, and witness the brith of a nation firsthand. The sights, sounds, and stories of Boston Harbour will transport you to an era of bravery and revolution.

Continental Congress Columns

Enter the hallowed halls of the Continental Congress, where the fate of a nation was decided. Engage with influential figures of the past, hear their impassioned debates, and witness the signing of the Declaration of Independence. Explore the exhibits and artifacts that showcase the stuggles and triumphs of those who forged the United States of America. Feel the weight of history as you stand where great minds shaped a nation.

Jersey

Embark on a thrilling adventure through the diverse landscapes of New Jersey, a section of our theme park dedicated to the monumental battles fought in the Garden State. Take a cruise down the Delaware, duel your fellow patriots, and fight in historic conflicts. Jersey has it all. Help the revolutionaries snatch victory from the jaws of defeat during the battles of Trenton and Princeton. Join us at some of the most pivotal moments of the Revolutionary War.

New York's Long Island

Step into the heart of the Revolutionary War at New York's Long Island, where many nail-biting moments of the Revolutionary War took place. Immerse yourself in the strategic battles that shaped the nation's destiny as you explore meticulously recreated battlefields, walk in the footsteps of courageous soldiers, and witness the resilience of the American forces. From interactive reenactments to captivating exhibits, experience the sacrifices and triumphs that marked this pivotal chapter in American history. Feel the spirit of liberty and honour as you delve into the dramatic events that unfolded on New York's Long Island during the Revolutionary War.

Yorktown

Prepare for an epic journey through the final chapter of the American Revolution as you explore the capitvating section of Yorktown. Engage in thriling reenactments, witness the decisive Battle of Yorktown, and experience the triumph of independence. Step foot on historic battlegrounds, interact with living history exhibits, and relive the heroic moments that secured America's freedom. Yorktown is a testament to the bravery and resilience of the American spirit.

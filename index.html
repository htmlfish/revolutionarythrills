About Revolutionary Thrills

Hello! Welcome to Revolutionary Thrills! We are a premier Revolutionary-War themed amusement park dedicated to providing entertainment and excitement for all ages. Our park features a wide range of attractions, from heart-pounding rollercoasters to immersive themed lands. At Revolutionary Thrils, we believe in life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness. Prepare for the adventure of a lifetime in our magnificent park! And don't forget to stick around after dark. You'll experience the true meaning of fireworks at Revolutionary Thrils! Our nightly spectacular will leave you in awe as cannons roar and rockets soar through the sky. We hope you enjoy our park. Have a revolutionary time!
